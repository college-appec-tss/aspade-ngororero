About the MEG Program

The MEG program at ASPADE Ngororero focuses on **Mathematics, Economics, and Geography**, providing students with analytical thinking, a deep understanding of economic systems, and environmental awareness. This prepares students for higher education and careers in business, economics, and global studies.

Core Subjects

Mathematics

Advanced mathematics courses including algebra, calculus, and statistics to develop problem-solving and logical thinking skills.

Economics

Learn how markets work, financial literacy, global economics, and decision-making skills to understand real-world economic challenges.

Geography

Study the environment, human geography, and sustainable development to build awareness of our world and global issues.

Projects & Research

Engage in practical projects, data analysis, and field studies to apply knowledge and enhance analytical skills.
