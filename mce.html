About MCE Program

The MCE program at ASPADE Ngororero combines Mathematics, Computer Science, and Economics to prepare students for higher education and the modern workforce. Students develop logical thinking, programming skills, and a strong understanding of economic principles.

Core Subjects

Mathematics

Learn algebra, calculus, statistics, and problem-solving techniques to build a solid mathematical foundation.

Computer Science

Develop coding skills in languages like C, C++, and Python, and explore algorithms, data structures, and software development.

Economics

Understand modern economic systems, financial literacy, global markets, and analytical thinking for decision-making.
