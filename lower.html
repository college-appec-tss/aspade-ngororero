Welcome to the O-Level Program

The Lower Secondary program at ASPADE Ngororero provides a balanced and engaging curriculum. Students explore sciences, languages, and humanities while developing critical thinking, creativity, and teamwork skills.

Core Subjects

Sciences

Explore Biology, Chemistry, and Physics through fun experiments and hands-on learning.

Languages

Develop strong communication skills in English, Kinyarwanda, and other languages to express ideas confidently.

Humanities

Learn History, Geography, and Social Studies to understand the world, cultures, and society.

ICT & Coding

Introduction to computing, basic coding, and problem-solving skills for the digital age.

Arts & Sports

Encourages creativity, team-building, and physical well-being through art, music, and sports activities.
